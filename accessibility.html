Accessibility Statement

Fried Chicken House is committed to ensuring that our website is accessible to all visitors, including people with disabilities. We strive to provide an inclusive digital experience that allows everyone to access our menu, find our location, and place orders with ease.

Our Standards

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA. These guidelines define how to make web content more accessible to people with a wide range of disabilities, including visual, auditory, physical, speech, cognitive, and neurological disabilities.

What We've Done

  • Sufficient color contrast ratios for all text and interactive elements
  • Keyboard navigation support throughout the entire site
  • Screen reader compatible markup with proper headings, landmarks, and ARIA labels
  • Skip navigation link for keyboard users
  • Descriptive alt text on all images
  • Clear indication when links open in a new tab
  • Minimum 44px touch targets for mobile users
  • Reduced motion support for users who prefer minimal animation
  • Semantic HTML structure with proper heading hierarchy

Known Limitations

  • The embedded Google Map may not be fully accessible to all screen readers. Our full address is provided in text next to the map for all users: 120W Washington St, Ste B, San Diego, CA 92103.
  • Some third-party content (social media icons, ordering platform) may have accessibility limitations outside of our control.
