Hillcrest

Fried Chicken House started with a simple idea: make real, crispy fried chicken the way it should be — hand-breaded, cooked fresh, and served with heart.

Nestled in San Diego's vibrant Hillcrest neighborhood, we've become the go-to spot for golden, juicy chicken that keeps people coming back. No shortcuts. No frozen. Just honest food made with care.
